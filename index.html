About Robotis

James Andrews and Jace Cooper founded Robotis LLC, a startup, with a focus on developing robotics solutions. We plan to develop a home care robot that can do basic tasks and chores around the house. Over the last year, we have completed our first stage of research on the high-level architecture of a social care robot which was published after the IEEE CCWC 2020 conference, where we won the best presenter of the session award.


Because the skill set of such a robot is so broad, we intend to identify a few key skill sets that people will want, and develop a platform that can be easily expanded to perform other tasks. To help us identify these key skill sets, we are reaching out to the community and asking for input.
